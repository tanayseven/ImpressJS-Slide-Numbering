Welcome to the demo of ImpressJS Slide Numbering

This is a slide numbering plugin for impress.js
As you have already seen there are slide numbers on the top left corner of the slide
These slide numbers are independent of the slide <div>s that to represent a slide
And, there is more to that...
You can jump to any slide you want by typing out the slide number
Type the slide number and then press enter, Eg. 4 then Enter
